MULTIPLAYER ROAD TRIP GAME

Rubberneckers Online

Rubberneckers Online is the e-version of the popular road-trip card game Rubberneckers. Now, you can play using a phone without the hassle of dealing cards, getting stuck with bad cards, or having to count up everyone's points at the game and pull out your phone's calculator like you're some kind of mongrel who doesn't know math (kidding). Anyways, I didn't want to count up points so I made the game do it for me.
